Welcome to Steganos – A Cybersecurity Symposium

We, the students of the Cybersecurity Department, are proud to present "Steganos", a symposium that dives deep into the unseen layers of the digital world. Inspired by steganography — the practice of hidden communication — Steganos reflects the true spirit of cybersecurity: smart defense through deep understanding. In today’s digital age, the importance of cybersecurity is undeniable. With rising threats and complex systems, learning how to protect digital assets has become a vital skill. This symposium brings that awareness to life through a mix of technical challenges, insightful sessions, and engaging activities. We’re hosting a range of core cybersecurity events along with common coding core events, offering opportunities for all participants — whether you're into ethical hacking, secure coding, or logical problem-solving. We welcome everyone who shares a passion for technology and security. Join us, learn something new, and be a part of Steganos — where innovation meets protection.
