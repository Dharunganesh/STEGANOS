Rules & Regulations

  • Each team must have 2 players
  • Teams will be paired with an IPL franchise and given an auction purse of ₹90 Cr.

  • Winners will be decided based on the stats of the chosen XI.
  • The judges’ decision will be final.
  • Winners & Runners-up will be rewarded.

Carry a valid college ID. Organizers’ decisions are final.
